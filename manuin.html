User Manual for Memory Game

Introduction

Test your memory and speed in this exciting digital game! Match the cards as quickly as you can before time runs out. Every second counts, so stay focused and improve your score with each round.

Features

Click on the cards to flip them and reveal their images. Find and match the identical cards. Complete all pairs before the time runs out. The faster you play, the more points you earn. Countdown timer to increase excitement. Have fun and challenge your memory!

Instructions

  1. Access the Memory Game

    • Open your web browser.
    • Navigate to the URL where the memory game is hosted (e.g., http://yourwebsite.com/memory).

    2. Once players press the start button, the timer will begin. Each player on the team must select 2 pairs of cards. If correct, they continue; if they fail, a teammate will take their turn.

    3. As they win, they will earn points and advance levels. The higher the level, the greater the difficulty and number of cards.

    4. The game can end in two ways: players complete all levels, thus finishing the game, or they fail, and time runs out.

    5. Once a team loses or wins, another team should take their turn. The team that wins in the shortest time will be the team with the best brain speed test.

    6. The cards may contain objects, fruits, emojis, etc. Each time a round progresses or finishes, the cards will be randomly shuffled.

    7. Each time a round ends, a message will appear on the screen indicating that the time is up and asking if they would like to try again. At the bottom, there will be a reset or start game button.
