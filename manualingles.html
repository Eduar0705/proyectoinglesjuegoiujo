User Manual for the Superhero Quiz

Introduction

The Superhero Quiz is an interactive game designed to test your knowledge of superheroes. You can choose from different difficulty levels and compete to achieve the highest score.

Instructions for Use

  1. Accessing the Quiz

    • Open your web browser.
    • Navigate to the URL where the quiz is hosted (for example, http://yoursite.com/quiz).

  2. Selecting Difficulty

    • Once the page loads, you will see a difficulty selection menu.
    • Choose one of the following difficulty levels:
      • Easy: Basic questions about superheroes.
      • Medium: Intermediate difficulty questions.
      • Hard: Advanced questions for true fans.

  3. Starting the Quiz

    • After selecting the difficulty, click on the corresponding button (for example, "Easy", "Medium", or "Hard").
    • The game will start, and you will be presented with the first question.

  4. Answering Questions

    • Read the question carefully.
    • You will see several answer options.
    • Click on the option you believe is correct.
    • After selecting an answer, you will receive immediate feedback:
      • If your answer is correct, you will see a message that says "Correct!".
      • If your answer is incorrect, you will see a message indicating the correct answer.

  5. Timer

    • You have a limited time to answer each question (for example, 10 seconds).
    • If time runs out, a message saying "Time's up!" will be displayed, and the correct answer will be revealed.

  6. Advancing to the Next Question

    • After answering, the game will wait 2 seconds before loading the next question.
    • Your score will be updated automatically.

  7. Ending the Quiz

    • The quiz will end when you have answered all the questions in the selected difficulty level.
    • A "Game Over" message will be displayed with your final score (for example, "Your score is: 3 out of 5").

  8. Restarting the Quiz

    • If you want to play again, click the "Restart" button.
    • This will take you back to the difficulty selection menu, where you can choose a new level or replay the same one.

Tips

  • Make sure to read each question and the answer options carefully.
  • If you are unsure of an answer, try to guess, as there is no penalty for incorrect answers.
  • Have fun and challenge your friends to see who gets the highest score.
